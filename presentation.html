üß† Q-Learning: The Learning Brain

The "Cheat Sheet" Analogy

  • Q-table = agent's "cheat sheet"
  • Stores value for every state-action pair
  • High Q-value = "This is a great move!"
  • Low Q-value = "Avoid this action!"
  • Initially all zeros (no knowledge)

Œ± (Alpha) - Learning Rate

How much new information overrides old.
High Œ± = Fast, unstable. Low Œ± = Slow, stable.

Œ≥ (Gamma) - Discount Factor

Importance of future rewards.
High Œ≥ = Long-term plan. Low Œ≥ = Short-term gain.

Œµ (Epsilon) - Exploration

Random vs. optimal actions.
High Œµ = Explore. Low Œµ = Exploit.

The magic is balancing immediate rewards (r) and future potential (Œ≥¬∑max Q), weighted by Œ±, while exploring with Œµ!
